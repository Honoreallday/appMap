Delali Azamati

Stanford University

As a member of many community based organizations, the knowledge I've gained was and continues to be crucial to my academic and intellectual develpment. Unfortunately CBOs are limited in the number of people they can reach, therefore I've assumed the responsiblity to share the knowledge I've been blessed with.

delali@stanford.edu

Yoona Oh

Rice University

As a student from a low-income family, I grew up knowing that paying for college was something that my parents could not do for me. However, as I got more familiar with college applications and scholarships, I realized that I had the ability to attend a prestigious university, and with the help of my mentors, friends, and QuestBridge, I got matched to Rice University on a full-ride. Now, as a rising college freshman, I want to share my experiences and knowledge with the next class of applicants.

j.oh@rice.edu

Emmanuel Honore

Vanderbilt University

College Possible and Minds Matter gave me the ins and outs to the whole college process, but even with that help it was still one hell of a ride. And without someone telling me what was what, and without all the support and mentorship they gave me, I'm not sure if i'd be where I am now. So I wanted to give everything I've learned back to the people who are getting ready to undergo the process themselves, so they know what there in for, and don't make the mistakes that come with being uniformed.

Emmanuel.k.honore@vanderbilt.edu
